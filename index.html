Common questions

How accurate are the rankings?+
We check rankings from Charlotte, NC (or your target city) using the same method Google uses for local searches. Results may vary slightly from what you see personally due to personalization, but our data is consistent and reliable for tracking trends.
How long until I see results?+
SEO takes time, but you'll start seeing your current rankings immediately. Most clients see meaningful movement in 60-90 days when following our weekly action items. The audit alone typically identifies quick wins that move rankings in 2-4 weeks.
Do you fix my SEO or just report on it?+
Stranex Rank is primarily a tracking and reporting tool. However, Stranex Solutions offers full SEO implementation as part of the complete website package. We'll tell you exactly what to fix — and we can fix it for you.
Can I track my competitors?+
Yes — on Pro and Agency plans, we track your competitors' rankings for the same keywords. You'll see who's outranking you, by how much, and what they're doing differently.
